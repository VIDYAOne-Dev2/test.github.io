About Maharajji

A Life Filled With Love & Compassion

Neem Karoli Baba, lovingly known as Maharajji, was a spiritual teacher from Uttar Pradesh, India. People from around the world were deeply touched by his kindness, simplicity, and unconditional love.

He spent many years traveling across North India as a sadhu, living a simple life and helping people through wisdom, compassion, and spiritual guidance.

Maharajji inspired the creation of many temples and ashrams, including the famous Kainchi Dham Ashram. Even today, millions of devotees remember him through prayer, service, and devotion.

“When you remember Maharajji with love and faith, his presence is always near.”

Neem Karoli Baba

Birthplace

Akbarpur Ashram

Uttar Pradesh, India

Akbarpur is the birthplace of Neem Karoli Baba Maharajji. This peaceful village in Uttar Pradesh is where Maharajji spent his early childhood. A beautiful temple with sacred murtis was later built here, making it a special spiritual place for devotees.

Sacred Birthplace
Temple & Murtis
Hanuman Sethu Mandir

Lucknow Ashram

Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

Inaugurated in 1967, Lucknow Ashram is one of the most visited Maharajji temples in the city. The temple is known for its peaceful atmosphere, beautiful Hanuman temples, and the daily distribution of Maharajji’s prasad to devotees.

Hanuman Mandir

Bhumiadhar Ashram

Uttarakhand, India

Located in the peaceful Kumaon Hills near Kainchi Dham, Bhumiadhar Ashram was one of Maharajji’s favorite places. He often stayed here and spent quiet time with devotees in the beautiful Himalayan surroundings.

Hanuman Mandir

Service & Devotion

Delhi Ashram

New Delhi, India

Delhi Ashram is known for its spirit of seva and devotion. Along with beautiful temples and prayer spaces, the ashram supports hospitals, schools, and daily food service for children and devotees.
